The secret to AI is not how it works, but how you talk to it.

Tip #1: Give AI a Persona

Tell the AI who it should be. For example: "Act as a professional coder" or "Act as a world-class basketball coach".

Tip #2: Use the "Role-Task-Format" Template

Try this structure for perfect results every time:

[Role]: Act as an expert history teacher.
[Task]: Explain the French Revolution to a 15-year-old.
[Format]: Use bullet points and simple language.
